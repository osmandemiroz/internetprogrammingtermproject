About Us

Established by Law No. 7141 published in the Official Gazette No. 30425 dated 8 May 2018, Eskişehir Technical University has 5 Faculties, 2 Vocational Schools, 1 College and 3 campuses in 3 campuses located in the center of Eskişehir, a city of science, culture and also youth. It consists of the Institute and 6 Research and Application Centers.

Show More/Less

Eskisehir Technical University works to prepare its students as the most equipped and competitive individuals for the future in creative and dynamic educational environments, with its units with a strong infrastructure where education and research activities are carried out, and with its competent faculty who follow the developments in their field and share all their time with their students. The operation of the international Hasan Polatkan Airport, located in the 2 Eylül Campus of our University, is carried out by our Faculty of Aviation and Astronautics. There are international flights to Hasan Polatkan Airport operated by different airline companies on various days of the week.
