About The University

Eskişehir Technical University, established by Law No. 7141 published in the Official Gazette dated May 18, 2018 and numbered 30425, consists of 5 Faculties, 2 Vocational Schools, 1 College, 1 School, 3 Institutes and 6 Research and Application Centers in 3 campuses located in the center of Eskişehir, a city of science, culture and youth. Eskişehir Technical University works to prepare its students as the best equipped and competitive individuals for the future in creative and dynamic educational environments with its competent teaching staff, each of whom follows the developments in their field and shares all their time with their students in its units with strong infrastructure where it carries out education and research activities. The international Hasan Polatkan Airport, located on the 2 Eylül Campus of our university, is operated by our Faculty of Aviation and Space Sciences. There are international flights operated by different airline companies to Hasan Polatkan Airport on various days of the week.

Vision and Mission

Vision

Eskişehir Technical University envisions a future where innovation, excellence, and social responsibility converge to empower individuals and transform communities. As a global leader in technical education and research, we aspire to inspire a new generation of forward-thinking problem solvers who shape the world through their creativity, expertise, and commitment to positive change.

Mission

Our mission at Eskişehir Technical University is to provide a transformative educational experience that equips students with the knowledge, skills, and mindset to excel in their chosen fields and make meaningful contributions to society. Through cutting-edge research, interdisciplinary collaboration, and engagement with industry and society, we seek to advance knowledge, drive innovation, and address the complex challenges facing our world. Guided by principles of excellence, integrity, and inclusivity, we are dedicated to fostering a vibrant learning community that nurtures curiosity, fosters creativity, and prepares students to thrive in a rapidly evolving global landscape.
